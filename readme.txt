Add an AI front desk to your site. EU-hosted.

== Description ==

Every visitor message is drafted by AI and approved by a person before it's sent. EU-hosted. English & Finnish.

== External Services ==

This plugin connects to the vaivatta service (https://vaivatta.fi), which is EU-hosted. Visitor messages are sent to and processed by the vaivatta service. See the Terms and Privacy Policy at https://vaivatta.fi.
